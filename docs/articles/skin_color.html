19 Years of Vogue US covers

Vogue magazine, one of the most successful magazines to have ever been published, was created in 1892. The magazine has a total readership of 1,398,00 and is accessible to all ages. It is considered as one of the most influential publications. Now acquired by Conde Nast, the magazine is specialized in beauty and etiquette for women. In 1974, Vogue became the first magazine to feature an African American model the cover. It is both a mirror and a shaper of model life.

“Vogue has the power to make and break – whether it’s fashion trends, designers, models, and yes, even industry practices.” - Tyra Banks
